Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip? +
We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips and 1-3 months for domestic travel. This ensures the best availability for flights and accommodations, especially during peak seasons.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Our cancellation policy varies by package. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund, 15-29 days receive a 50% refund, and less than 15 days are non-refundable. Please check your specific package details.
Do you offer travel insurance? +
Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all international trips. We partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage for trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost luggage.
Can you accommodate special dietary requirements? +
Absolutely! We work with our partners to accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences. Please inform us of any requirements during the booking process so we can make the necessary arrangements.
